New York New Student Party

Saturday, July 15 | 2:00 - 4:00 p.m.

Hosted by Sue Matteson King '85 P'18, David King P'18, Danny King '18, and Diana King
Rye, NY 10580 (specific address will be emailed to you upon registration)

Please join us as we welcome our newest students and their families into the CMC community. These informal and relaxed gatherings provide a great opportunity for alumni, parents, and current students to meet the new generation of CMCers. For new students and families, this is your chance to become acquainted with the CMC community, get to know others in your area, and have your questions answered by those who have gone before you!

Casual attire; refreshments will be served.
